The Find it Fund it Philly Map Tool uses the Vulunerability Index created by the PREACT Team. PREACT (Planning for Resilience and Equity through Accessible Community Technology) is a multipurpose and multi-scalar climate preparedness and neighborhood planning organization in the city of Philadelphia. The grants were complied by the Find it Fund it Philly team.
